Courses for Faculty of IT

Technology Foundation Programme ( CINEC Campus )

Our Engineering pathway will help you develop the technical and mathematical abilities you'll need to develop new technologies and structures. You will gain a robust foundation in engineering fundamentals as well as a fundamental understanding of mathematics and problem-solving strategies through this curriculum, which will prepare you to move to bachelor's degree programs in engineering. CINEC Campus will offer you a strong theoretical and practical basis while also helping you enhance your occupational and life skills throughout this foundation program which is offered by the University of Wolverhampton in UK.

Entry Qualifications

5 Credit Passes for GCE (O/L) Sri Lanka /London including Credits for Mathematics, Science and English

  • 3 passes for Advanced Level in a single attempt for core subjects (Local or International equivalent) (London AS Level exams are not recognized as entry criteria)
  • Completion of NSBM foundation programme
  • Completion of Edexcel or BTEC Certified foundation programme (*Subject to the approval of program director)
  • Students should have passed Ordinary Level examination or equivalent including Mathematics and English

BSc (Hons) Computer Science ( Wolverhampton -United Kingdom )

The course aims to produce software engineers, software developers, programmers and systems Engineers who can seamlessly make the transition from University to the International Computer Industry. We will provide you with a thorough grounding in the core principles of computer science and integrating these with computer languages, tools, techniques and methodologies used by computer professionals worldwide. Additionally, we will prepare you with the key skills needed to keep abreast of future developments in the exciting career to which this course leads.

Entry Qualifications

  • G.C.E. A/L Any Stream 1C and 2S passes (Local) or 1C, 2D (London) + 5 Credit Passes for O/L (London /Local) including Credits for Mathematics Science and English
  • Foundation + 5 Credit Passes for O/L (London /Local) including Credits for Mathematics Science and English
  • Any other qualification as approved by the University of Wolverhampton

Job Opportunity

A computer science software engineering degree is a highly respected qualification which can lead you into careers such as; Software Developer, Programmer, Software Engineer, Systems Engineer and Quality Assurance Specialists.

Full Time Duration 3 Years

Bsc (Hons) Information Technology Specialising in Computer Systems & Network Engineering

The programme aims to provide students with the knowledge, skills, and comprehension required to allow them to contribute to the planning, design and management of modern network-based computer systems. The degree programme delivers an extensive knowledge on computer hardware, network engineering, embedded systems, communication systems, interface design and automation.

Course Structure

You will complete a dissertation on a topic that you negotiate with your tutor. There is a high level of personal research and independent working, under supervision from your tutor. Your progressed will be assessed through assignments and your dissertation. Compared to an undergraduate course, you will find that this Masters course requires a higher level of independent reading and research.

Entry Qualifications

  • Passes in three subjects including Physics and Combined Mathematics at the G.C.E. (Advanced Level) examination (or equivalent subjects at the London G.C.E.(Advanced Level) examination) in one and the same sitting and pass the Aptitude Test conducted by SLIIT.

Course Fee

The fee is presently Rs 220,000 per semester. Fees for any subsequent semester should be paid prior to the commencement of each semester. This all inclusive fee is charged to cover lectures, tutorials and examinations and access to computer laboratory facilities and library.

Job Opportunity

A computer system & network engineering degree is a highly respected qualification which can lead you into careers such as; Network Administrator, Network Engineer, Network Designer, Database Engineer, Infrastructure Manager, System Designer, Network Architect & Network Programmer.

Full Time Duration 4 Years
