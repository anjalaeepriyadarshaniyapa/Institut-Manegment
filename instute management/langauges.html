Certificate Course In German Language ( NIBM )

By the end of the programme the participants will be able to communicate in German in day-to-day communication with basic writing, reading and listening skills

Entry Qualifications

Students should have either of following qualifications

  • hose with no prior German knowledge or those who have acquired a basic knowledge.

Certificate In English For Professional Purposes ( Northshore College )

This course is designed to enable students to be fluent, effective and confident in communicating in English in their day-to-day and professional environments while enhancing their language skills to be competent in using the language for different purposes.

Entry Qualifications

Professionals, public/private sector employees and any others who are interested can follow this course upon fulfilling:

  • GCE Ordinary Level with a Credit pass or above
  • for English, Applicants will also be eligible to follow this course based on their performance at an Admission Test.

Course Fee

Registration Fee: LKR. 2,000/-
Programme Fee: LKR 25,000/-


Full Time Duration 4 Months
